Admission


Admission Requirements for Kinder 1 and 2

Application for Kinder 1 and 2 for the new school year are made in the months of November until January.

Requirements:

1. Age Requirement

    *Kinder 1: 4 1/2 to 5 years and 5 months by june 1 of the academic year.
    *Kinder 2: 5 1/2 to 6years and 5 months by june 1 of the academic year

2. Photostat copies of the following:

    - Birth Certificate ( Authenticated by NSO)
    - Baptismal Certificate
    - Alien certificate of registration ( ACR for foreigners only ) study permit from the immigration office

3. Other requirements

    - Personal Date sheet issued by SFA to be accomplished in duplicate copies
    - Three recent ID pictures ( 1x1 )
    - Entrance examination fee
    - Certification of previous schooling ( for K2 only )
    - Medical and dental examination records

Admission Requirements for Grade and Year Level

Acceptance for applications starts on January to April of the year before the applied academic year.

Requirements:

1. Age requirement for Grade 1: 6 1/2 to 7 and five months by june 1 of the academic year.

2. Personal Date sheet issued by SFA to be accomplished in duplicate copies

3. Birth certificate ( Authenticated by NSO )

4. Baptismal Certificate

5. Certification of schooling ( For Grade 1 only )

6. Report card with the following final grade requirements:

    ( FOR GRADE 1 TO 5 )
    - No final grade lower than 78% in any subject.
    - A Final average of at least 80%
    - A conduct of department grade of at least B or its numerical equivalent
    - No failing grade in any period ( quarter, trimester, semester )

7. A letter of recommendation from the principal/guidance counselor

8. Two recent ID pictures (1x1)

9. Entrance examination fee

10. For foreign students: a study permit or a verified copy of the alien certificate of registration ( ACR ) , if accepted.

11. Medical and dental examination records

Enrollment Procedures

Release of Examination Records

The entrance test result is released on the date specified by the school. The applicant's parent / authorized representative can claim it form the SFA office.

Enrollment of Old Pupils/Students

The enrollment of old pupils / students for the new school year is determined by the SFA principal's office. The school reserves the right to accept a new pupil / student to take the place of an old pupil . student after the official enrollment period.
