Vision-Mission Statement

St. Francis academy - De La Salle Supervised is an educational institution in Dasmariñas, Cavite supervised by the LASSO (Lasaliian schools supevision office). Inspired by the teaching of St. Francis of Assisi and St. John Baptist de la Salle, the school patron saints, St. Francis Academy De Lasalle Supervised envision itself to be the school of future achievers for God and Country. Energized by SFA core values, the students acquire knowledge and understanding of their role in our world characterized by rapid changes in technology and globalization.

A. To provide a strong foundation of basic education in the Lasallian tradition of religious, mores at culture that will prepare the student for independent living and life learning.

B. To nurture an environment characterized b a sense of communion and respect for individual differences human dignity and diversity.

C. To develop the child spirituality character and moral values in the light of the life and teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ.

D. To instil social responsibility and strengthen national pride trough a sense of belongingness to Filipino culture and tradition.

E. To encourage a great awareness of the qualities of a Franciscan learner- in simplicity, humility and purity.

F. To inspire the students to follow the spirituality of St. John Baptist De la Salle in commitment and service to poor.
