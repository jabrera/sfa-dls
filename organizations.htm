Varsity Teams

St. Francis Academy will form teams of Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, Table Tennis and Chess to compete in Inter-La Salle Competition and the annual staging of the CISAA meet scheduled from September to December every Saturday at La Salle-Dasmarinas, cavite.

A try-out is being held every summer to select the final compositions of the teams, followed by the training during school days and Saturdays.

Student Body Organization (S.B.O.)

At the start of the school year, an election is being held to choose the officers of the student body. An organization which is expected to lead the school activities being lined-up for the school year, this group of pupils/students are task with great responsibilities trusted in them by the pupils/students.

La Sallian Diario

The La Sallian Diario is designed to provide pupils/students an annual overview of the paper. The objective is to familiarize pupils/students with newspaper content and writing styles, so that it may serve as a source of information and entertainment. It supplies facts, photos and illustrations of various students insights and activities. It also contains editorials, letters, columns and opinions of its staff and readers.
