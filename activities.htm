Activities


The Computer Program

The St. Francis Academy Computer Program is designed to develop computer literate pupils/students whose knowledge of the program will enable them to use the computer and all its relevant facets in learning. Being part of the general curricular program, some subject areas will use the computer in the teaching-learning processes. From Kinder 2 to Year IV, pupils/students will be taught basic technology skills such as word processing, electronic presentations, graphics and web designing and internet use. Incorporated therein is the advocacy to the basic ethical principles in handling technology.

The Penmanship Program

The St. Francis Academy Penmanship Program is a component of the Language Program. The program aims to develop the pupil/student's handwriting skills in print and in cursive, following the Lasallian style of handwriting. Penmanship lessons are offered in ALL grade levels, starting Kinder 1 as early as the second quarter of first grade, pupil/students will be trained to write in cursive strokes, following the prescribed format. This style is embedded in every Lasallian curricula, is part of the child's holistic formation. Every SFA-DLS graduate is expected to exhibit excellent Lasallian handwriting.

Co-Curricular Activities

On the Spot Drawing Contest/Poster Making & Slogan

The annual contest, held on the week of the celebration of the school's foundation, aims to challenge the creativity and artistry of selected pupil/students from the different grade levels. Categorized into two: primary and intermediate, pupils/students with in the category are pitted against each other producing outcomes, based on a topic presented only then.

Interpretative Reading Contest (English and Filipino)

As early as the first quarter of each year the Grades 1 to Year 4 Reading, Filipino and Language teachers monitor the pupils/students progress in reading and in appreciating good literature. Selected pupil/students who are gifted in interpreting short stories are given the opportunity to utilize their intelligence, voices, imagination and emotions in giving life to a selected of a variety of literature.

Spelling Contest

In Developing word skills, the pupils/students are further encouraged to develop their spelling skills. The pupils/students' training in spelling will help them to further enhance their language, reading and communication skills. Periodic contest will be held in each class from Grades 1 to 5. Top spellers of each grade level will be identified in each quarter, and placed on the Principal's List of Achievers.

Creative Writing Contest

In Developing the pupils/students' skills in writing literary pieces, semestral selections of creative writers is held per class. Each pupil/student is given an opportunity to express his/her creativity and ideas about a given topic. Winning pieces shall be posted on the School's Bulletin Board, and likewise published in the Semestral School Paper.

Speak English Campaign

As early as Kinder 1, pupils/students will be encouraged to communicate in the English language, in all circumstances, within the school premises. The year-long campaign aims to develop among the pupils/students the capacity to express themselves in the universal language, with ease and competence, symbolic of their being Lasallians.
