July 11, 2011 - Varsity Training

Saint Francis Academy - De La Salle Supervised would like to inform you that we will be joining the Dasmariñas City Meet on selected events and the Cavite Inter-Secondary School Athletic Meet on the following events: Basketball, Volleyball (boys & girls), Badminton (boys & girls), Table Tennis (boys & girls), Chess (boys & girls). This is a part of our sports program lined up for this school year 2011-2012.

In connection with this, we will be having our regular practices during weekdays and Saturdays and the actual games of CISAA/DPSAA (schedules) which will be given later.

Basketball (Monday & Wednesday - 3:30pm to 5:00pm / Saturday - 8:00am to 11:00am)

Volleyball (Tuesday & Friday - 3:30pm to 5:00pm / Saturday - 8:00am to 11:00am)

Badminton/Table Tennis/Chess (Thursday - 3:30pm to 5:00pm / Saturday - 8:00am to 11:00am)

Thank you for your untiring support and consideration.

Please approach Sir Nestor for Reply Slip.
