July 11,2011 - Field Trip

Field Trips expand students' learning through active hands-on experience with the rich resources of our local community. Also, it increases the students' knowledge and understanding of a subject and adds realism to the topic of study.

Hence, with these goals, Saint Francis Academy - De La Salle Supervised will be having an Educational Field Trip on the 23rd of September, 2011 and will be exploring the Manila Ocean Park by all levels from Elementary to High School. In the said park includes the Oceanarium, Jellies, Fish Spa, Glass Bottom Boat Ride, Koi Feeding, Bird Photo, Wizard of Oz Stage Play and Musical Fountain Show.

Venue accommondation, air conditioned bus, tour guide, meal (lunch) and insurance for each participant will be covered by the Php 1,550.00, educational trip fee. Parents and guardians are most welcome to join and accompany their children.

Kindly fill up the slip given to you and submit it to your adviser before July 29, 2011.

As part of our tour management, the seat selection will be on the first come, first served basis. Payment will be due on or before August 31, 2011.

Please approach your adviser for Reply Slip.
