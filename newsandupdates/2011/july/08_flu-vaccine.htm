July 8, 2011 - Flu Vaccine

We are pleased to inform you that on August 23-25, 2011, we will be having our medical and dental check-up for the students to be held in the school premises to be conducted by Dr. Ruel S. de Guzman and Dra. Chinkee S. Natividad. In addition to this, Glaxo - Smith Kline, in partnership with the school, plans to administer flu vaccine to the students. This is very important for the protection and productivity of our school populace to prevent absenteeism.

Moreover, influenza occurs year-round in the Philippines but most of the diseases occur during the rainy season like the months of July and August. It is therefore recommended that yearly flu vaccination be given to both children and adults for continued protection. This is also for parents/guardians who wish to decrease the chance of catching flu. The vaccine costs 380 pesos each.

We are conducting a survey to find out how many will avail of the vaccine. Kindly answer the reply slip. Thank you very much.

Please approach your adviser for Reply Slip.
