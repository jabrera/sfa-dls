August 8, 2011 - Mathematics for Teachers Assistance Program

          In line with the school's plan to uplift the mathematics competencies of all high school students, Saint Francis Academy will be participating in the 2011 Department of Education or DepEd - MTAP (Mathematics for Teachers Assistance Program). The program aims to provide the students the opportunity to do Mathematics with explorations without the threat of test and an intensive review of the work of previous year and the first few weeks of this year.

          The said program will run for six (6) Saturdays, the dates are as follows: August 27, September 10 and 17 and October 1, 8 and 15. Second year and Third year students will attend the morning session from 8:00am to 12:00nn and First year and Fourth year students will attend the afternoon session from 1:00pm to 5:00pm.

          The MTAP fee is Php200.00 to be paid to their class adviser on or before August 25, 2011. The fee will be used for the materials and other expenditures of the said program.

Please approach your adviser for Reply Slip.
