June 30, 2011 - July and August schedules

Welcome to school year 2011-2012. We are honored that you have chosen St. Francis Academy to be your partner in educating your child. This year is geared towards strengthening our on-going school campaigns which are: the English Speaking Campaign which will prepare and make our students be more competent in the mastery of the English language. Furthermore, the From Clean Words to Courtesy and Clean as You Go campaigns will instill the values of respect, accountability and responsibility towards fellow students and school personnel as well as the environment.

This coming July 1, 2011, we will be having our First Friday Mass at 9:00am. We offer this mass for a blessed and productive year ahead of us as well as the celebration of Br. Felipe C. Bellaza's 25th year as a Lasallian brother. The preschool pupils maybe accompanied by their guardian/s; primary pupils will be hearing the mass from their classrooms. Intermediate and high school students are required to attend in their gala uniform. This mass is sponsored by the administration and faculty.

As part of the security measures implemented by the school, private vehicles and vehicles used as carpool are required to use stickers. They are available at the administration office - Php 350.00 (private vehicles) and Php 800.00 (carpool). Vehicles with no stickers will not be allowed to enter the school premises starting July 11, 2011.
