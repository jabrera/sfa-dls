September 23, 2011 - Field Trip Guidelines

Guidelines for Ocean Park and Enchanted Kingdom:

1. Be in your P.E. uniform - Bring extra plain WHITE or any SFA T-shirt.

2. Assemble Time - 6:00am.

3. Departure Time - 6:30am. PLEASE BE ON TIME

    Manila Ocean Park - 8:00 to 12:00nn
    Enchanted Kngdom - 2:00pm to 7:00pm

4. Estimated time of arrival in SFA - 8:00pm

5. No other gadgets allowed. Only cellphone be permitted.

6. Board your designated bus. No transferring of buses. Take note of the bus number and plate number.

7. Always go with your group - An assigned tour guide for each bus will be provided.

8. Dong't get reckless on the bus. You don't want your day to end early, do you? Misbehavior on the bus can get you into trouble and ruin your day. You could end up sitting on the bus while the others enjoy the destination.

9. Don't wander off. Listen carefully when the teachers gives instructions about sticking with the group or sticking with an assigned partner-even when going to the restroom. Don't ever wander off on your own, or your trip could end badly.

10. Respect nature. Some field trips will take you into contact with animals or plants. For your own safety, be mindful of potential dangers and don't assume you can tug, pull, tease or touch things safely.

11. No roughhousing. You may visit a factory containing equipments with moving parts, or a museum with rooms full of pottery and glass, or a riverside with fast-running water. Kids don't always think about dangers that come with certain places, so think about the potential hazards before you go, and remember not to pust or pull your friends.

12. Keep an eye on the clock. If you are supposed to meet your group for lunch or for loading onto the bus, you should keep an eye on the time. You don't want to miss lunch and you surely don't want to be left behind.

13. Eat and drink in designated places. Don't assume you can buy soda from a machine and drink it anywhere. Your destination site might have strict limitations when it comes to drinking or eating on site. Remember CLAYGO - Clean as You Go.

14. Bring a small notebook. Take down important notes.

15. Pay attention to the speakers. This trip is for your education.
