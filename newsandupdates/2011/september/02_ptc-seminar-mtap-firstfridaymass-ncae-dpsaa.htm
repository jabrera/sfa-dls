September 2, 2011 - PTC/ Seminar/ MTAP/ First Friday Mass/ NCAE/ DPSAA

1.) On September 3, 2011, we will be holding our Parent - Teacher Conference/Distribution of Cards for the first quarter, from 9:00 am to 11:00 am. We encourage you to participate in this activity so you may be able to get an update of your child's performance in school as well as get to know his/her adviser. Also grade six, first and second year high school students will be going to the Cultural Center of the Philippines to watch the epic play "Hinalawod" The play is a source of the information about culture, religion and rituals of the ancient people of Sulod. It will give us a glimpse of ancient Filipinos' belief in the "sacred", importance of family, honor, courage and dignity. It is hoped that watching this play will foster nationalism and pride for Filipino culture.

2.) To promote campus journalism, selected elementary and high school students will take part in two - day private schools media convergence (seminar - workshop on campus journalism) on September 2 and 3, 2011. This seminar will equip our students with the necessary skills for individual writing contests and putting up a school paper.

4.) On September 8, 2011, we will be celebrating the birth of our Blessed Mother. This mass will be sponsored by our graduating elementary students. We request the following: 1 piece of bath soap and I sachet of shampoo from the elementary pupils and 1 toothbrush and 1 sachet of toothpaste from the high school students. The goods collected will be given to our selected communities our outreach activities.

5.) As per Department of Education Memo No.153, s. 2011, the National Career Assessment Examination (NCAE) will be given on September 28, 2011 to the following:

    a.) Third year high school students instead of fourth year high school students

    b.)Fourth year high school student - applicants of CHED and TESDA Scholarship Programs

To help the students perform better, review classes will be given on September 10 and 17, 2011. We encourage all third year students to attend.

6.) We are also proud to announce that our high school students participated in the DPSAA (Dasmariñas Private School Athletic Association). All of them played well and as a result of it, the Table Tennis Team (Boys) won the championship and our Taekwondo Jins won three gold medals. They will be representing Dasmariñas in the City Meet 2011. In addition, we will also join the opening of the CISAA (Cavite Inter-Secondary Schools Athletic Association). These endeavors will teach our students the value of sports , hard work , discipline and sportsmanship.
