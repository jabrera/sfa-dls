September 26, 2011 - Foundation Day and Annual Sports Festival

As part of the celebration of our 8th Foundation Day, Saint Francis Academy - De La Salle Supervised will be having the Annual Sports Festival during the Foundation Week which will be held on November 14 to November 19, 2011.

The school decided to combine these two events to give our students more time to attend their academic needs. In line with this, all students are required to have their jersey uniform which they will use during Games and Cheer Dance Competition/ Presentation as well.

All high school students and intermediate pupils were divided into Four Colored teams - with each team having players from the different levels: Grade 4 to Grade 6 pupils for Intermediate and; 1st to 4th Year students for high school.

The cost of the jersey uniform is Php 630.00 - You can pay the jersey at the Administration Office starting Monday, October 3 until November 4, 2011.

We hope that you will support our activities lined-up for our 8th Foundation Day Celebration.
