November 3, 2011 - MTAP/ Campus Journalism/ Foundation Day and Sportsfest/ PTC

As the years comes to an end, we would like to once again extend our sincerest gratitude for the support and trust you have given to us. Please be informed of the following:

  1. We would like to thank you for supporting MTAP (Mathematics Teacher Association of the Philippines) - DepED Saturday Program. Your continuous support is vital for enrichment/remedial classes will be conducting in the future.

  2. In the 2011 Private Schools Media Convergenceheld last September 2 & 3, 2011, we are proud to announce that we ranked 5th overall in the elementary division and 6th overall in the high school division among the different private school in Cavite. In the Campus Journalism contest participated by both public and private schools, five of our students qualified to take part in the regional competition this November. With these developments, we will further push to strengthen and improve the journalistic skills of our students.

  3. This coming November 14-18, 2011, we, the SFA community will be celebrating our 8th Foundation Day and Sports Festival with the theme: Strengthening School Spirit: Through Sports and Recreation. The objectives of these activities are to promote sports as well as strengthen and deepen our sense of unity. Joining these two major school events will maximize our time as well as lessen expenses. We invite all of you to take part and witness this simple yet significant activity. November 14, 2011 marks the beginning of the celebration with a mass on November 16, 2011.

  4. We invite you to attend the 2nd Quarter Parent - Teacher Conference on November 12, 2011, from 9:00 a.m. - 11:00 a.m. It is important that you get an update on your child's progress in school.

  5. Lastly, please be guided with our summary of activities/schedules for the months of November, December 2011 and January 2012. Click here to open school calendar.
